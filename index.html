About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Josh Grimes, a data scientist with a focus on business analytics. Over the past eight years, I worked as a teacher, using data to drive better outcomes in education. To further enhance my skills, I recently completed an intensive data science program, and I’m excited to combine my expertise as an educator with the analytical tools I’ve developed.

Before my career in education, I served in the U.S. Air Force as an Air Transportation Supervisor, where I trained junior airmen in the safe and efficient transport of passengers and cargo on military aircraft. This experience honed my leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills.

Outside of work, I’m passionate about sports analytics (go NC State, Carolina Hurricanes, Carolina Panthers, Washington Nationals, and Charlotte Hornets!), endurance running (I’ve completed four marathons), and gaming (I’m ranked in the top 2.5% worldwide in Fortnite).
